Manga Style

2D Illustration

Manga style is a type of comic book style that originated in Japan. It is characterized by its distinctive art style, which features large eyes, small mouths and exaggerated expressions. The stories in manga comics often include a wide range of genres, including action, romance, science fiction, and fantasy. They are typically read from right to left, unlike Western comics which are read from left to right.
